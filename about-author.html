About the Author

Meet the expert behind the simplest guide to public speaking

Favour Ebi Dakoru

Communication Expert & Public Speaking Coach

Favour Ebi Dakoru is a renowned communication expert, public speaking coach, and inspirational speaker with over 15 years of experience in helping individuals and organizations master the art of effective communication.

With a passion for empowering others to find their voice and speak with confidence, Favour has trained thousands of professionals, students, and leaders across various industries. Her unique approach combines practical techniques with psychological insights, making public speaking accessible to everyone.

As a sought-after keynote speaker, Favour has addressed audiences at major conferences, corporate events, educational institutions, and churches worldwide. Her engaging style and practical wisdom have made her a favorite among audiences seeking to improve their communication skills.
