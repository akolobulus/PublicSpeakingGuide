Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get support for my book purchase?

If you're having trouble with your digital download or need help with your order, please email us at officialarikpa@gmail.com with your order details.

How do I book Favour for a speaking engagement?

You can use our "Book the Author" form on the website or email officialarikpa@gmail.com with details about your event, preferred dates, and audience size.

Do you offer bulk discounts for organizations?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders (10+ copies). Please contact us directly to discuss your needs and receive a custom quote.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all purchases. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Is there a study guide or additional resources?

Yes, the book includes bonus worksheets and templates. We also have additional resources available on our website for book owners.

How can I leave a review or testimonial?

We'd love to hear about your experience! You can submit your review through our contact form or email us directly. We may feature your testimonial on our website.
